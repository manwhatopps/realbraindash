# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2026

## 1. Introduction

BrainDash ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address, phone number, username
- **Identity Verification:** Government-issued ID, date of birth, address (for Cash Play)
- **Payment Information:** Payment method details (processed by third-party processors)
- **Communications:** Messages sent to customer support

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** Game performance, session duration, features used
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, browser type
- **Location Data:** IP address, approximate geographic location
- **Analytics:** App interactions, crash reports, performance data

### Information from Third Parties
- **Social Login:** Profile information if you sign in with Google or Apple
- **Payment Processors:** Transaction status and payment verification

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### Provide and Maintain the Service
- Create and manage your account
- Process game entries and prize payouts
- Verify your identity for Cash Play
- Provide customer support

### Improve the Service
- Analyze usage patterns and trends
- Fix bugs and improve performance
- Develop new features and content
- Test and optimize user experience

### Ensure Fair Play
- Detect and prevent cheating
- Identify fraudulent activity
- Enforce our Terms & Conditions

### Communications
- Send account-related notifications
- Provide customer support responses
- Send promotional materials (with your consent)

### Legal Compliance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Respond to legal requests
- Prevent illegal activities

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

### Service Providers
- **Payment Processors:** Stripe for payment processing
- **Identity Verification:** Third-party KYC/AML verification services
- **Cloud Hosting:** Supabase for data storage
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, crash reporting tools

### Legal Requirements
- Law enforcement when required by law
- Court orders or legal processes
- Protection of our rights and property
- Investigation of suspected violations

### Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption:** Data in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Limited employee access to personal data
- **Security Audits:** Regular security assessments
- **Secure Infrastructure:** Industry-standard hosting and databases

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### Access and Portability
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Export your data in a machine-readable format

### Correction and Update
- Correct inaccurate personal information
- Update your account details

### Deletion
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Right to be forgotten (with exceptions for legal compliance)

### Objection and Restriction
- Object to certain processing of your data
- Restrict how we use your data

### Withdraw Consent
- Withdraw consent for marketing communications
- Opt-out of optional data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@braindash.com

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:
- Your account is active
- Required to provide the Service
- Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

After account deletion, we may retain certain data for:
- Legal compliance (tax records, transaction history)
- Fraud prevention and security
- Dispute resolution

## 8. Children's Privacy

BrainDash is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## 10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:
- Remember your preferences
- Analyze usage patterns
- Provide personalized content
- Support authentication

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.
